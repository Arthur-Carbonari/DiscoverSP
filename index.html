Discover SP

São Paulo, Brazil’s vibrant financial center, is among the world's most populous cities with more than 12 million people living in its capital and one of the largest conurbations in the world, where more than 22 million people live.

With numerous cultural institutions and a rich architectural tradition, São Paulo is one of the few cities in the world that truly never sleeps, and where you can find everything from everywhere.

With a rich and diverse history, São Paulo is heavily packed with cultural attractions and stunning nightlife, there are no regrets when you know where to go.

The Coffe Boom

XXI Century

First settled in 1554, São Paulo became an official city in 1711. With a very rich history, its main development started in the second half of XIX century due the construction of Santos-Jundiai railway that facilitated the path of the coffee beans of São Paulo to the Port of Santos. Since then São Paulo has become the main economic city of Brazil and South-America and the most populated one.
